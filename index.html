About me

I am a postdoctoral scholar at the Institute of Computer Graphics and Vision of Graz University of Technology (TUG). I received my Ph.D. degree with distinction from TUG, under supervision of Prof. Vincent Lepetit, in 2018. Before that, I obtained my B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees in Computer Science from EPFL, Switzerland, in 2012 and 2014. My research interests include machine learning methods for computer vision, specifically methods for 3D scene understanding, 3D object pose estimation, 3D hand pose estimation, hand-object interaction, semi supervised learning, and domain adaptation.
